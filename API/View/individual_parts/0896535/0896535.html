Here, you can check up on the CHIBB house using a visualisation.

This visualisation shows the temperature in each room of the CHIBB house.
The room colours go from dark blue (the coldest) to bright yellow(the warmest), as you can see in the legend.
You can mouse over the rooms and legend to see what the exact temperature is.
