Here, you can check up on the CHIBB house using visualisations.

This visualisation shows the temperature in each room of the CHIBB house.
The room colours go from dark blue (the coldest) to bright yellow(the warmest).
You can mouse over the rooms to see which room it is, and what the exact temperature is.
